## 🔹 Investigation Methodology Used

Across labs and case studies, I follow a structured approach:

1. Identify the alert or suspicious activity  
2. Collect relevant logs and telemetry  
3. Analyse patterns and anomalies  
4. Map findings to known attack techniques  
5. Assess severity and business impact  
6. Recommend containment or escalation steps  
7. Document findings clearly and professionally  

This mirrors real SOC workflows used in enterprise environments.
